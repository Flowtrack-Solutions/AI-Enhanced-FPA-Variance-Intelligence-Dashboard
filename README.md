# 🤖 AI-Enhanced FP&A Variance Intelligence Solution v1.1

Transforming traditional FP&A reporting into AI-powered financial intelligence and executive decision support.

---

## Overview

AI-Enhanced FP&A Variance Intelligence Solution is an executive-level Power BI solution designed to enhance Financial Planning & Analysis through variance intelligence, forecasting insights, strategic risk monitoring, and AI-inspired decision support.

The solution provides finance leaders with a centralized view of budget performance, operational variance drivers, forecast outlook, risk exposure, and executive recommendations to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives

* Monitor Budget vs Actual vs Forecast performance
* Identify operational variance drivers
* Analyze department-level risk exposure
* Generate AI-inspired executive insights
* Support strategic forecasting decisions
* Improve financial planning visibility
* Enhance management decision-making through intelligent recommendations
